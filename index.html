Where Am I?

You wake up in a complete, velvety, blackness. You don't know where you are.

You hear a voice..... No wait, you don't hear it. It's kinda like hearing except the sound is coming from inside your brain......

"Shhhh, don't be troubled traveler. There is nothing to fear. You are inside another's mind. My mind. To get out, you must explore. Every nook and cranny must be felt, else there will be no escape. Find the star. Take the star. Then you shall be rewarded. Heed my words traveler, and remember them well..... "

Suddenly, three separate floating texts appear in front of you.

They read:
